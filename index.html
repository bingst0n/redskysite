Red Sky Sports Academy LLC is the premier multi-sport day camp located at The Cape Cod Academy in Osterville, Massachusetts. Red Sky offers a wide variety of activities including soccer, lacrosse, volleyball, basketball, baseball, tennis, street hockey and flag football. Although taught in groups each sport is catered to the individual camper's ability.

We welcome beginners to sport, those working on developing their skills and youngsters who simply want an enjoyable camp experience.

Our half-day program is a good way to introduce young people to the camp format and allows seasoned campers time to enjoy the rest of their busy summer schedule with family and friends.
