Red Sky Sports Academy

About

Mission Statement

Red Sky Sports Academy LLC is dedicated to the personal and moral growth and development of young people through sport and physical activity.

Facility Description

Red Sky Sports Academy is a multi-sport camp for boys and girls ages 5-14 located in beautiful Osterville, Massachusetts at The Cape Cod Academy.

History

Red Sky Sports Academy LLC formerly known as Soundview on Cape Cod is an outgrowth of Soundview Sports Camps of New York which opened its doors in the summer of 1995. Red Sky owner and director Blair Moynahan is the son of Steve Moynahan, founder, owner and director of Soundview in New York.
