Our Director

Blair Moynahan, owner and director of Red Sky Sports Academy LLC, holds a BA in recreation management from Springfield College and a Masters in Education from Hofstra College. He is currently pursuing his second Masters in Administration. Blair started his camp career as head lacrosse coach when Soundview Sports in New York opened its doors in 1995. He quickly went on to become the assistant director. In 2004 Blair became director and owner of Soundview at Sacred Heart University. In 2009 Blair founded Soundview Cape Cod, now Red Sky Sports Academy LLC.

During the school year Blair is a physical education teacher in the Tarrytown school district in Westchester County, NY. He is the head coach of girls' varsity soccer and lacrosse as well as assistant track coach at Rye High School.

Blair is an avid athlete. Beginning with little league, athletics have been a large part of Blair's development. In high school Blair was a three season varsity athlete who captained a state finalist football team and still holds a school record in track. At Springfield Blair was a valuable member of the men's varsity lacrosse team that went to a national championship.

Blair loves Cape Cod. He, his wife Meghan and their three children spend all their summers in Craigville.

Our Staff

The Red Sky staff is comprised of professional athletes, high schools coaches and college and high school athletes. While all staff members are highly proficient in their sports, they understand that each camper comes with his/her own set of abilities. They use challenging yet attainable athletic goals to foster the love of sport and teach campers how to make good choices both on and off the playing field.

Red Sky also has a certified trainer on the campus at all times. The trainer is equipped to deal with medical emergencies, treat minor injuries and administer medication. All head coaches are CPR and first aid certified.
