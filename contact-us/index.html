Directions

From the Mid-Cape Highway (Route 6)

Take exit 5 (Route 149). Drive south for approximately .1 mi. until the road forks. Keep left at the fork towards Osterville. Continue for approximately 3.5 miles, crossing Race Lane (at the blinking light take a quick left, then a quick right) and Route 28 (stop light). The school is on the left, 1/2 mile beyond the Route 28 intersection.

From Route 28

Turn south off Route 28 onto Osterville-W. Barnstable Road (Mahoney's Garden Center is on the corner). CCA is 1/2 mile down, on your left.
